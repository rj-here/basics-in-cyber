Passwords

Consider strong passwords. Long and random, not something simple like 123456. Look into a password manager.

VPN

A strong consideration is to get a VPN, which stands for Virtual Private Network. This encrypts traffic and protects you on any network. That is partiuclarly helpful on an unfamiliar network, such as a coffee shop in Downtown Toronto. Without a VPN, someone could actively be monitoring your activity as you "quickly pay off" the credit card bill due in 2 days.

Security Check

Be sure to take a look at where you are logged in, which third-party services are connected and more.
